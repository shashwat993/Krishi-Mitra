Latest Government Schemes & Programs

Kisan Credit Card Loan Scheme 29.11.2017

The KCC scheme was introduced to ensure that the credit requirements for farmers in the agriculture, fisheries and animal husbandry sector were being met. This was done by providing them with a credit limit to purchase equipment and for their other expenses as well.

Soil Health Care Scheme 15.06.2019

A Soil Health Card is used to assess the current status of soil health and, when used over time, to determine changes in soil health that are affected by land management. A Soil Health Card displays soil health indicators and associated descriptive terms.

Fasal Beema Yojna 20.10.2019

To provide insurance coverage and financial support to the farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crop as a result of natural calamities,pests & diseases and to stabilise the income of farmers to ensure their continuance in farming.
